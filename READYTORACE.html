FIX IT

In this event, the contestants will be provided with dismantled parts of RC car and inorder to acquire this parts each contestant has to complete 3 tasks which are as follows:
1). Identification of Sensors:
It involves identification of provided sensors exactly.
2).Circuit Arrangement:
Contestant is provided with a logic diagram which should be implemented practically on a breadboard.
3).Blind Coding using Python:
The contestant has to program without the presence of a monitor and without any errors.
The contestant who completes all these 3 tasks will be provided with the parts of RC car and he/she should assemble it and race through the track and the one who completes the event within the shortest time possible wins the game.
